Our Story

A legacy of luxury since 1998

Where Elegance Meets Warmth

Founded in 1998, Grand Luxe Hotel was born from a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where world-class luxury meets genuine warmth. Nestled in the vibrant heart of the city, our hotel has welcomed discerning travelers from around the globe for over two decades.

Every detail, from the hand-selected artwork adorning our halls to Egyptian cotton sheets on every bed, reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence. We believe luxury isn't just about opulence; it's about how you feel the moment you walk through our doors.

Our dedicated team of hospitality professionals ensures that every stay is not just comfortable, but truly memorable. Because at Grand Luxe, you're never just a guest, you're family.
