From relaxation to recreation, Grand Luxe offers an exceptional range of amenities designed to elevate your moment of stay.

Surrender to serenity in our full-service spa. Enjoy rejuvenating massages, facials, and bodybtreatments performed by expert therapists using premium products. Our tranquil oasis is designed to melt away stress and leave you feeling refreshed and renewed.

Rooftop Infinity Pool

Take a dip high above the city in our stunning rooftop infinty pool. Enjoy paranomic skyline views while our poolside attendants serve refreshing cocktails and light bites. Whether you're lounging on a sunbed or swimming under the stars, our rooftop pool offers the perfect blend of relaxation and luxury.

Savour exquisite cuisine at our award-winning restaurant, where our executive chef crafts seasonal menus using locally sourced ingredients. Unwindnat our elegant bar with handcrafted cocktails and fine wines.

Stay active during your stay in our fully equipped fitness center. Featuring the latest cardio machines, free weights, and strength training equipment, our gym is designed to meet the needs of every fitness level. Personal training sessions and group classes are also available to help you achieve your wellness goals.
